VIRAT & ANUSHKA

Two Souls,
One Destiny

What began as a chance meeting blossomed into the most beautiful love story either had imagined. Virat and Anushka's journey is one of laughter, growth, and an unshakeable bond that feels written in the stars.

"In all the world, there is no heart for me like yours. In all the world, there is no love for you like mine."

From the busy streets of Mumbai to the serene ghats of Varanasi, their love story crossed cities, seasons, and every test that life offered. Today, they stand together — ready for forever.

2013

First Meeting

A cricket match in Mumbai brought them together. She caught his eye; he caught her heart. Neither knew it was the beginning of everything.

2015

The Slow Fall

Late night conversations, surprise visits, stolen moments between schedules — two lives quietly rearranging themselves around each other.

2016

Instagram Official

A single photo confirmed what the world already suspected. The comments section went into a frenzy — the couple just smiled.

NOV 2017

The Engagement

Under a canopy of stars in Tuscany, Virat knelt and asked the question they both knew was coming. She said yes before he finished the sentence.

DEC 2017

Forever Begins

December 11th, 2017. In the presence of family, friends, and centuries of tradition, Virat & Anushka begin their eternal chapter together.
